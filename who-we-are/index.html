Who We Are

We represent the Santa Cruz chapter of the system-wide campaign known as Fossil Free UC.

We are students from varying disciplines, backgrounds, hometowns, and identities --- fighting together against intersectional injustice. We are both learners and leaders, empowering each other to reframe societal narratives and utilize our voices in the face of climate change.
