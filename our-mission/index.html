Our Mission

We, Fossil Free UCSC, demand full divestment from fossil fuels, advocate for system change, challenge traditional hierarchies, create a brave space for people of all identities, stand in solidarity with all communities fighting for justice, check our privileges, build student power, turn outrage into agency, respect the need for self care, acknowledge varying involvement capacities, denounce white supremacy, uplift marginalized voices that have been systemically silenced, recognize climate justice as social justice, assume responsibility for our actions, reframe societal narratives, reflect upon and adapt our campaign, open our minds to alternative ideas and perspectives, empower and love one another, refuse to accept inevitability, and remain committed to our goal of creating a globally just and sustainable society for present and future generations of humanity.
